# Rubric: privilege-handling@1

**Kind**: legal · **Gate**: every artifact; strictest on litigation matters · **HITL on fail**: yes (Table I)

## Must-haves (fail if absent)
- Work-product banner present; on dispute matters, anticipation-of-litigation expressly invoked.
- No privileged content written to scopes broader than the matter's grant (memory, cross-squad envelopes, broad output paths).
- Litigation-hold trigger evaluated on any dispute matter (issued / not-triggered, stated).

## Scored dimensions (0–1; pass ≥ 0.7 all, revise ≥ 0.5 any, fail < 0.5 any)
| Dimension | Weight | What good looks like |
|---|---|---|
| Channel discipline | 0.35 | Privileged analysis stays in privileged artifacts; distribution minimal; third-party waiver risks flagged |
| Mixed-purpose separation | 0.25 | Legal analysis separated from business advice; the trap named where present |
| Boundary redaction | 0.25 | PII/privileged facts redacted at squad/system edges (Table X) |
| Label accuracy | 0.15 | Banners match content (no banner-washing of non-privileged material) |

## Verdict envelope
`pass` / `revise` / `fail` (+ Tribune's Veto rendered).
