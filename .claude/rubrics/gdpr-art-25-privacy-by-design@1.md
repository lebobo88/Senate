# Rubric: gdpr-art-25-privacy-by-design@1

**Kind**: privacy · **Gate**: matters with personal data in scope · **HITL on fail**: yes

## Must-haves (fail if absent)
- Lawful basis named for every processing purpose (or its absence flagged as a finding).
- Data minimization analysis present ("could less data achieve the purpose?" answered honestly).
- Cross-border flows have a transfer mechanism analysis (or a halt verdict).

## Scored dimensions (0–1; pass ≥ 0.7 all, revise ≥ 0.5 any, fail < 0.5 any)
| Dimension | Weight | What good looks like |
|---|---|---|
| By-design integration | 0.30 | Privacy measures designed into the activity (defaults, pseudonymization, access control), not appended as afterthought |
| Necessity & proportionality | 0.25 | Purpose limitation explicit; retention bounded; subject-rights mechanics addressed |
| Risk assessment quality | 0.25 | Likelihood × severity scored; special-category and vulnerable-subject multipliers applied |
| Anonymization honesty | 0.20 | The three-part test applied to any "anonymized" claim; pseudonymized called pseudonymized |

## Verdict envelope
`pass` / `revise` / `fail` (+ Tribune's Veto rendered).
